Void Commander

Dual-panel file manager for macOS power users. Fast, keyboard-first, local-only. Work with two directories side-by-side—one as source, one as destination. Copy, move, and manage files with single keystrokes.

Queued Copy & Move

Handle large transfers with progress tracking, pause/resume/cancel. Per-item conflict resolution or apply to all. No interruptions, full control.

Multi-Rename Tool

Token-based batch renaming with live preview. Regex replace, case conversion, sequential counters. Undo stack and savable templates.

Advanced Selection & Find

Wildcard masks, range drag, invert selection. Recursive filename search with regex, depth control. Results in navigable panels.

Previews & Archives

Inline image/text previews (F3), cross-panel Quick Look (F1). Pack/unpack archives with progress and error handling.

Network & Customization

Mount SMB/NFS/AFP shares directly. Full hotkey rebinding, themes, typography. Activity panel for live operations.

How It Works

Step 1: Dual Panels

Open two directories side-by-side. Navigate with keyboard arrows, Tab to switch panels.

Step 2: Select & Act

Use advanced selection tools—wildcards, ranges, invert. Copy/move with F5/F6 or custom keys. Queue jobs in Activity panel.

Step 3: Preview & Rename

Hit F3 for inline previews, F1 for Quick Look. Batch rename with live preview, regex, templates.

Step 4: Customize & Mount

Rebind every key, switch themes. Mount network volumes directly. All settings exportable as JSON.
